Visual

If you are a visual learner, you learn by reading or seeing pictures. You understand and remember things by sight. You can picture what you are learning in your head, and you learn best by using methods that are primarily visual. You like to see what you are learning. As a visual learner, you are usually neat and clean. You often close your eyes to visualize or remember something, and you will find something to watch if you become bored. You may have difficulty with spoken directions and may be easily distracted by sounds. You are attracted to colour and to spoken language (like stories) that is rich in imagery.

Here are some things that visual learners like you can do to learn better:

  • Sit near the front of the classroom.
  • Have your eyesight checked on a regular basis.
  • Use flashcards to learn new words.
  • Try to visualize things that you hear or things that are read to you.
  • Write down key words, ideas, or instructions.
  • Draw pictures to help explain new concepts and then explain the pictures.
  • Colour code things.
  • Avoid distractions during study times.

Project Idea

Overview

The project is to create a virtual reality multiplayer online role-playing game. This will provide a more realistic experience for gamers who play multiplayer online role-playing games and will revolutionise how we play online. The game that I would like to create would be based off the light novel and anime series Sword Art Online.

Motivation

I have a passion for playing multiplayer online role-playing games, the social aspect of it and just getting lost in a fantasy world where you can be and do just about anything. Ever since I saw the Anime Sword Art Online, I have always dreamed of one day being able to play a virtual reality multiplayer online role-playing game.It would be a great achievement to be part of the development for such a game. Currently to date there are around 3 billion people in the world that play video games, so I think something like this will be the next best thing.

Description

To play the game you will need a Pimax Vision 5K Super VR headset which is compatible with thousands of VR games today and a PC to run it all. The game will be playable with a $30 monthly subscription. The game will consist of three Regions, Oceanic, North America and Europe. Given how popular World of Warcraft is there will be several servers implemented in each region to compensate for the vast number of players to be expected. The game will include an in-game shop where you will be able to purchase some services like faction change, race change and character transfers.

The game will still take place in the world of Azeroth, having two continents: the Eastern Kingdoms and Kalimdor, with each continent split up into 10 different leveling zones. There will be two factions to choose from either Alliance or horde, both containing four races to choose from: humans, dwarves, gnomes, and night elves for the Alliance and orcs, trolls, tauren, and the Forsaken for the Horde to choose from and being able to pick one of the nine classes to play as. Amongst these classes you will be able to choose the role of Tank, Healer or Damage dealer. Each race will have a different starting zone and have their own capital city along with a king/war chief.

There will be two types of game modes to choose from, Player vs Player and Player vs Environment. Player vs Player will not be activated while in the races starting zone and will activate once you hit level 10 regardless of where you are. You have the option to deactivate and activate the Player vs Player mode, but you must be in a Capital city to do so. You will be able to tell if someone has this mode activated by a crossed swords symbol next to their name and you can only attack someone of the opposing Faction.

Once in game you will be able to start your adventure throughout Azeroth questing and leveling up. To begin with there will be a level cap at level 50, but once at level 50 you will gain access to end game content such as raids. Apart from end game raids you will have access to various dungeons throughout your travels as you level up. The first dungeon will be accessible at level 10 where you will be able to form a party with other players consisting of a tank, healer and 2 damage dealers. As you progress and level up you will unlock more dungeons to explore.

The game will also have a regular maintenance schedule and be down once a week, this will allow for any updates and to rectify any issues at hand. The future timeline for this VR design will have multiple major expansion packs as well as extra in game features to be added. The expansion packs will be aimed to come out every four to five years.

Tools and Technologies

The game will be developed using Unreal Engine 5. Unreal Engine is a top choice for developing this game due to what can be achieved, it has photoreal rendering in real time, a robust multiplayer framework, VFX and particle simulation, advanced AI, film quality post-process effects, unreal audio engine and limitless extensibility.

Skills Required

There are several skills that will be required to make this game for example:

  • Programming – In charge of developing the code required for the game, developing a website and the creation of the installer. They will need a basic knowledge about scripts and C++ programming language.
  • Sounds – Responsible for music, sound effects and overall audio for the game.
  • Design – In charge of maintaining, updating the game design and connecting all the various parts of the project.
  • Quality assurance – Responsible for playing the game in order to find bugs, glitches and any other issues. This task can be done in house or done by beta testers.
  • Business – In charge of the games marketing, sales, subscriptions and funding.
  • Art - They will oversee the visual elements you will see while playing the game and making sure they meet the specifications.
  • Project management – Oversees the overall game development, making sure each team knows the task at hand and the time frame allocated.
